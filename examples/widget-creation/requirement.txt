Article page showcase widget:

- allows a marketer to select 1-4 article pages
- has 2 presentation templates - Full and Minimal. Full is more of a fancy, art-designed presentation that works best for a single article and minimal is better for multiple articles.
- has a presentation option to show or hide the teaser image
- has a presentation option to show or hide related articles
- retrieves all linked content (related articles, images)
- displays articles in the order that they were selected
- uses the Site.css styles and matches the design of the existing components
- handles the following scenarios
  1. the selected articles are missing from the database (should show a message to the marketer only)
  2. the articles have no linked image (should show a placeholder)
- should use the correct Xperience by Kentico APIs for webp page content retrieval and caching, URL generation for links to articles and their linked image assets
